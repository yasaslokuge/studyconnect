About StudyConnect

StudyConnect is a platform designed to bridge the gap between international students and the local community. We understand that international students often have diverse skills and are looking for opportunities to earn income while studying.

Our platform allows students to offer their services in various categories, from cleaning and tutoring to tech support and creative services. At the same time, it helps local residents and businesses find reliable, affordable services.
